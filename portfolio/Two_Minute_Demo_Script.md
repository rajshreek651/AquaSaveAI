# AquaSave AI - Two-Minute Demo Script

## 0:00-0:20 - Introduce the challenge

“Water is frequently wasted through small leaks and unusual usage that people notice only after receiving a higher bill. My project, AquaSave AI, helps a household or small building identify potential wastage early.”

## 0:20-0:45 - Show the data and dashboard

“This dashboard uses 14 days of simulated hourly water-use data for a four-person household. It shows total use, average daily use, potential waste, and the number of alerts. The daily trend helps the user see when consumption increases.”

## 0:45-1:15 - Show the smart solution

“The rule-based detector looks for continuous overnight flow and unusual usage compared with the normal pattern for the same hour. It detected a possible overnight leak and unusually high garden use. Each alert explains the reason and gives a practical action, such as checking toilet flush tanks or reducing watering duration.”

## 1:15-1:35 - Show AI and impact

“I also added a lightweight Isolation Forest that learns from 90 days of generated normal-use data. It compares AI anomalies with the explainable rules. The impact section translates detected waste into estimated water, cost, pumping energy, and CO2 savings.”

## 1:35-2:00 - Demonstrate scale and conclusion

“Users can upload their own CSV and download a report. For scale-up, the same workflow can receive smart-meter or IoT flow-sensor readings from hostels, apartments, or campus buildings. AquaSave AI turns water data into understandable conservation action and supports SDG 6.”
